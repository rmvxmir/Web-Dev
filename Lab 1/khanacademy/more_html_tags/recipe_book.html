#1 - Korean Pop Chicken

  • Time: ~40 mins
  • Serves: ~20 mins
Ingredients Quantity
Chicken 500 g
Corn starch 1/2 cup
Salt 1 tsp
Garlic Powder 1 tsp
Oil (for frying) 1 tbsp
Korean sauce 250 ml

Step 1: Prep the Chicken - Mix cornstarch, flour, salt, and garlic powder. Coat chicken pieces in the mixture.
Step 2: Fry - Heat oil in a pan. Fry chicken until golden and crispy. Set aside.
Step 3: Coat Chicken - Toss fried chicken in the sauce. Serve hot, topped with sesame seeds and green onions if desired.
